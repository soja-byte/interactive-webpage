Your Weekly Workout

3-Day Full Body Routine

Day 1 - Monday: Strength Focus

Goblet Squats 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 7

Hold a dumbbell vertically against your chest with both hands. Stand with your feet slightly wider than shoulder-width, toes pointing slightly out. Push your hips back and squat down, keeping your chest up and back straight. Go as deep as comfortable, then drive through your heels to stand back up.

Incline Dumbbell Press 3 sets | 8-10 reps | RPE 7

Set an adjustable bench to a 30-45 degree incline. Lie on the bench, holding a dumbbell in each hand at your shoulders. Press the dumbbells up and together over your chest. Lower them slowly and with control to the starting position.

Seated Cable Row 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 8

Sit on a cable row machine with a neutral-grip handle. Keep your back straight and lean forward to grab the handle, keeping a slight bend in your knees. Pull the handle towards your belly button, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Slowly extend your arms back to the starting position.

Plank with Reach 3 sets | 20 sec per side | RPE 6

Start in a standard plank position on your hands or forearms, with your body in a straight line from head to heels. While keeping your hips stable, lift one arm and extend it forward. Hold for a moment, then return to the start and repeat with the other arm.

Walking Treadmill (Incline) 10-15 min | RPE 6

Set a treadmill to an incline of 7-10%. Walk at a steady, brisk pace. This is a great way to warm up or cool down, and it's easier on your joints than running.

Day 2 - Wednesday: Cardio + Upper Body

Lat Pulldown (wide grip) 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 7

Sit at the lat pulldown machine and grab the wide bar with an overhand grip. Pull the bar down towards your upper chest, squeezing your lats. Slowly return the bar to the starting position with control.

Machine Shoulder Press 3 sets | 10 reps | RPE 7

Sit in the machine with your back firmly against the pad. Grip the handles and press the weight up until your arms are fully extended. Lower the weight slowly and with control.

TRX or Cable Face Pulls 3 sets | 15 reps | RPE 6

Grab a rope attachment on a cable machine set to face height. Pull the handles towards your face, pulling your hands apart as you do so. Squeeze your rear delts and upper back, then slowly return to the start.

Seated Leg Curl 3 sets | 12-15 reps | RPE 7

Sit on the machine and adjust the leg pad to rest on your lower calves. Curl your legs down towards the machine, contracting your hamstrings. Slowly return to the starting position.

Stairmaster Intervals 5 x 1 min work : 1 min rest | RPE 7-8

Alternate between a 1-minute high-intensity sprint (RPE 7-8) and a 1-minute rest or slow walking pace. Repeat for a total of 5 work periods.

Day 3 - Friday: Hypertrophy + Conditioning

Leg Press 3 sets | 12-15 reps | RPE 8

Sit in the leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart on the platform. Lower the platform slowly by bending your knees. Stop when your thighs are at a 90-degree angle to your shins, then press back up.

Dumbbell Chest Press 3 sets | 10-12 reps | RPE 8

Lie on a flat bench, holding a dumbbell in each hand at your chest. Press the dumbbells up until your arms are fully extended. Lower them slowly and with control back to the starting position.

Cable or Dumbbell Lateral Raises 3 sets | 12-15 reps | RPE 7

Stand holding a dumbbell in each hand or a cable handle in one hand. Keeping a slight bend in your elbow, raise your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the floor. Slowly lower them back down with control.

Bicycle Crunch 3 sets | 20 reps total | RPE 6

Lie on your back with your hands behind your head and your knees bent. Crunch your torso up while bringing your left elbow to your right knee, extending your left leg. Alternate sides in a smooth, pedaling motion.

Rower Intervals 4 x 250m | 60 sec rest | RPE 8

On a rowing machine, row at a high-intensity pace for 250 meters. Rest for 60 seconds, then repeat for a total of 4 rounds. Focus on a powerful leg drive to generate speed.
